Drone Training Center Equipment Rental Portal

Welcome to the Drone Training Center Equipment Rental Portal. The Drone Training Center is a technology-focused organization that supports practical drone learning, flight training, and hands-on technical development for its members. It provides access to useful equipment for training activities, project work, testing, and technical exploration in a structured and professional environment.

Our center is designed for members who need specialized drone-related tools and devices for practical work. Depending on availability, members may reserve equipment such as training drones, flight controllers, FPV goggles, drone batteries, and drone simulator kits. These resources help members complete learning activities, improve operational skills, and explore drone technology in a safe and organized way.

This reservation portal allows members to book equipment online by submitting the required personal and reservation details. After the form is submitted, the system will display all entered information for review. Members may then go back to revise the information or confirm the reservation.

After confirmation, the system will verify whether the reservation is made between 24 and 72 hours in advance. If the reservation falls within this required time period, the system will display a thank you page. If the reservation is too soon or too far in advance, the system will display a sorry page explaining the problem.

Please complete the reservation form carefully and ensure that all information is correct before submitting your request.

What We Offer
The Drone Training Center provides members with access to drone-related equipment for training, experimentation, project work, and technical development activities.
Equipment Available for Reservation
Members may reserve equipment such as training drones, flight controllers, FPV goggles, drone batteries, and drone simulator kits, subject to availability. Each reservation request allows the member to apply for one item only.
Reservation Process
Members submit the online reservation form, review the entered information, and then confirm the booking. The system will validate whether the selected reservation time is between 24 and 72 hours in advance.
Important Reminder: Please ensure that all submitted information is accurate and check all reservation details carefully before pressing the Reserve button. Incorrect reservation details may affect the booking result during the confirmation stage.
